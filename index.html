About To-Do Good

Due to the increasing amount of time spent using mobile devices, the proponent has come up with a solution through developing a mobile to-do list application of religious acts that aims to improve religious connection of its users. It encourages to improve the consistency of them doing their religious tasks. Religious tasks are generated and added to the user's to-do list. The tasks generated will vary depending on the user's selected religion during account creation. In addition, the application will also include avatars and levels. Lastly, some minor game aspects are also included for users to have fun while trying To-Do Good.
